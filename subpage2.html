This is the logo I made on Illustrator for JSOC, or Journalism Students of Color. Learning to use Illustrator was difficult.
We learned Photoshop first and I was surprised that Illustrator is a lot different.
I made this logo by typing the letters with a font I liked and then morphing the shapes.
I chose colors that complimented each other and to play in to how they contrast, I tried to make a drop shadow.
This assignment was a bit difficult because of how I'm unfamiliar with Illustrator.
But, I'm glad I know some basic functions in case I find myself wanting to use Illustrator in the future.
